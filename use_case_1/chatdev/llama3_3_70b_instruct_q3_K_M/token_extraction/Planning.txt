The interactivity can be enhanced with links, animations, and transitions, though overuse can detract from the content. Ultimately, the most suitable modality depends on your specific needs, the type of information you're presenting, and how you intend to engage with your audience. Combining modalities—using images within documents or PowerPoint presentations, for example—can also offer a comprehensive approach to communication.
